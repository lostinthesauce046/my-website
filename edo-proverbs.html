Edo Proverbs and Wisdom

Wisdom of the Edo People

The Edo people have a rich tradition of proverbs that convey wisdom and life lessons. Here are a few:

  • "A child who is not embraced by the village will burn it down to feel its warmth."
  • "He who learns, teaches."
  • "The man who uses force is afraid of reasoning."
