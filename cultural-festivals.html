Cultural Festivals

Egyptian Festivals

Egyptians celebrate various festivals, including:

  • Ramadan: A month of fasting and spiritual reflection.
  • Eid al-Fitr: A festival marking the end of Ramadan.
  • Sham el-Nessim: A spring festival celebrated with picnics and outdoor activities.

Edo Festivals

The Edo people celebrate several important festivals, such as:

  • Igue Festival: A celebration of the new year and the king's reign.
  • Osun-Osogbo Festival: A festival honoring the river goddess Osun.
