Famous Egyptian Figures

Cleopatra

Cleopatra VII was the last active ruler of the Ptolemaic Kingdom of Egypt, known for her intelligence and political acumen.

Ramses II

Ramses II, also known as Ramses the Great, was a pharaoh of the Nineteenth Dynasty, known for his military leadership and monumental building projects.

Imhotep

Imhotep was an ancient Egyptian polymath who served as chancellor to the pharaoh Djoser and is credited with designing the Step Pyramid.
